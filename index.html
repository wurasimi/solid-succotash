What is IAF?

The International Academy of Forensics (IAF) is an independent organisation instituted to provide innovative research and development, and world-class professional career development in Forensic Investigation and Criminal Intelligence, with application to the administration of justice. Globally, the Academy collaborates with high impact institutions to offer specialised training programmes in the field of knowledge.

WHAT WE DO

Forensics Investigation and Criminal Intelligence

Forensic Investigation and Criminal Intelligence focus on gathering and analyzing evidence to solve crimes. They help identify suspects and support law enforcement with factual insights.

Forensic Accounting and Criminal Intelligence

Forensic Accounting and Criminal Intelligence involve investigating financial records to detect fraud and criminal activity. They help law enforcement uncover hidden transactions and track illicit funds.

Digital forensics and cybersecurity

Digital forensics and cybersecurity focus on investigating cybercrimes and protecting digital systems. They help identify security breaches, recover data, and prevent cyber threats as well as cyber fraud.
